Who we are?

Connecting Sri Lanka’s brightest creative minds

Mission

To empower local creatives by offering a collaborative space for sharing, learning, and growth.

Vision

To be the leading platform that showcases Sri Lanka’s creative potential to the world.

Values

Creativity | Collaboration | Empowerment | Cultural Pride

Our Story

The Sri Lankan Creative Network was born out of a desire to bring together the vibrant and diverse creative minds of Sri Lanka. As the world becomes more interconnected, we saw an opportunity to create a platform that fosters collaboration, inspiration, and growth for artists, designers, and creatives of all kinds.

Our mission is to provide a space where members can showcase their work, share ideas, and connect with like-minded individuals to drive the future of creativity in Sri Lanka. We believe in the power of community and that through collaboration, we can achieve greater success and push the boundaries of what is possible in the creative industry.
